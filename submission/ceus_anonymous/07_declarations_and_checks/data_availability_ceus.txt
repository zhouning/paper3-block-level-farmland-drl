Data and code availability

The original Third National Land Survey cadastral parcel data are subject to access restrictions and cannot be redistributed. Aggregate statistics, derived block-level metrics, training logs, model artifacts, evaluation outputs, figure-generation scripts, and code required to validate the reported results are available for double-anonymized review at https://anonymous.4open.science/r/block-level-farmland-drl-8456/ and will be made available to readers through a public repository after acceptance.
